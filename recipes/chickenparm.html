Chicken Parm

Description:

A recipe for Chicken Parmesan explains how to make a crispy, cheesy chicken dish baked with tomato sauce. The chicken breasts are pounded thin, coated in seasoned flour, egg, and breadcrumbs, then fried until golden. They are placed in a baking dish, topped with marinara sauce, mozzarella, and grated Parmesan cheese, and baked until the cheese melts and bubbles. The dish is typically served hot with pasta or bread, creating a rich, savory meal with a crispy texture and tangy tomato flavor. 🍗🍅🧀

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 7 ounces seasoned bread crumbs
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 12 ounces pasta sauce
  • 6 slices Monterey Jack cheese
